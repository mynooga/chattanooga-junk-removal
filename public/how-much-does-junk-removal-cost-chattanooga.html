Junk removal costs vary, but most jobs in the Chattanooga area fall within a predictable range. Here's what you need to know before you call — and how to get the best value.

The Short Answer

Most junk removal jobs in Chattanooga cost between $75 and $600, depending on the size and type of the load. Small jobs — a few items, a chair, a mattress — start around $75–$125. Full truck loads for large garage cleanouts or estate cleanouts can reach $400–$600 or more.

The key thing to know: you won't find a one-size-fits-all price for junk removal. Any reputable company gives you a quote based on your actual job. See our pricing page for a more detailed breakdown, or just call and we'll estimate it for you over the phone in minutes.

What Affects the Cost of Junk Removal

Volume: The single biggest factor. More junk = more truck space = higher cost. Smaller loads pay proportionally less.

Item type: Heavy materials like dirt, concrete, or tile cost more per unit volume due to disposal fees. Standard household junk is priced lower.

Accessibility: Items on the curb or in an open garage are quickest to load. Upstairs items, items in tight spaces, or jobs requiring disassembly take more time.

Special disposal items: Things like tires, certain electronics, or refrigerators with refrigerants sometimes carry additional fees due to proper disposal requirements. We'll always tell you about these before we start.
