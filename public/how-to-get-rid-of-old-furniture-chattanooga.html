Your Options for Getting Rid of Old Furniture in Chattanooga

There's more than one way to get furniture out of your home. The right choice depends on the condition of your furniture, how fast you need it gone, and how much effort you're willing to put in.

Option 1: Donate It

If your furniture is in decent condition — no major stains, tears, or broken parts — donating it is a great option. Chattanooga has several organizations that accept furniture donations, including Habitat for Humanity ReStore locations. The catch: most donation centers require you to drop it off yourself, and they often decline pieces that are worn, stained, or heavily used. If your furniture has seen better days, this likely won't work.

Option 2: Sell It

Listing furniture on Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, or Nextdoor can work for items in good shape. You might recover some of your money, but it takes time — listing it, responding to inquiries, scheduling pickups, and dealing with no-shows. It's also on you to get it out of the house once someone buys it. If you're in a hurry or the furniture isn't selling, this becomes frustrating quickly.

Option 3: Curbside Bulk Pickup

Chattanooga offers bulk item pickup through city services, but it requires scheduling in advance, has limitations on what they'll accept, and may mean leaving items on the curb for days. It's free, but it's not always fast or convenient — and not all items qualify.

Option 4: Hire a Junk Removal Company

The fastest, easiest option regardless of the furniture's condition. You call, we show up, we load it and take it away — usually same day or next day. No listings, no waiting for buyers, no dragging things to the curb. We handle furniture in any condition and from anywhere in the home, including upstairs. See our pricing to understand the cost.
